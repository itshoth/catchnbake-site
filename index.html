Overview

About This Policy

Catch 'N Bake ("we," "us," or "our") is a mobile application that helps anglers identify fish species from photos, log their catches, and discover recipes. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

By using Catch 'N Bake, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Data Collection

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the app's core features:

  • Account information — name and email address when you sign in with Apple
  • Photos — images you submit for fish identification (processed and not stored long-term)
  • Catch log data — fish species, location notes, and catch details you choose to record
  • Usage data — basic app analytics to improve performance and fix bugs
How We Use It

Use of Your Information

We use your information solely to operate and improve Catch 'N Bake:

  • Identify fish species from photos using AI image analysis
  • Authenticate your account and maintain your session securely
  • Store and display your personal catch log and history
  • Improve app performance and fix technical issues

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Services We Use

Catch 'N Bake uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — secure user authentication and database storage
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI-powered fish identification from photos
  • Apple Sign In — secure, privacy-preserving authentication

Each of these services has their own privacy policy and data practices. We encourage you to review them.

Your Rights

Data Retention & Deletion

You are in control of your data:

  • You may delete your catch log entries at any time within the app
  • You may request full account deletion by contacting us
  • Photos submitted for identification are not permanently stored on our servers
Security

How We Protect Your Data

We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), secure token-based authentication via Supabase, and no storage of sensitive credentials on device beyond encrypted session tokens.

Children

Children's Privacy

Catch 'N Bake is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
